Who We Are

Our Focus & Approach

CA Ramesh Baheti is a seasoned Chartered Accountant with a strong track record in delivering practical, compliant, and value-driven financial solutions to businesses and individuals across diverse sectors.

With deep expertise in taxation, audit & assurance, accounting, regulatory compliance, and strategic advisory, the firm is built on a foundation of technical excellence, integrity, and client centricity. Our approach goes beyond routine compliance—we focus on risk mitigation, financial optimisation, and long term sustainability for our clients.

Over the years, CA Ramesh Baheti has advised MSMEs, professionals, family businesses, and growing enterprises, helping them navigate complex regulatory frameworks with clarity and confidence. Every engagement is handled with precision, confidentiality, and a clear understanding of commercial realities.
