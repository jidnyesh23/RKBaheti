Our Scope

Financial Advisory Services We Offer

Strategic financial services to drive growth, stability, and informed decision-making

Financial Planning & Analysis

Strategic financial planning, budgeting, forecasting models, and variance analysis to help management make data-driven operational and strategic decisions.

Working Capital Management

Analysis of working capital cycle, cash flow projections, debtor management, inventory optimisation, and strategies to improve liquidity and operational efficiency.

Bank Loan & Project Finance

Preparation of project reports, CMA data, financial projections, and liaison with banks and financial institutions for term loans, working capital, and project financing.

Business Valuation

Independent business valuation for M&A transactions, shareholder disputes, ESOP schemes, regulatory requirements, and investor due diligence purposes.

Business Restructuring

Financial restructuring advisory for businesses facing challenges — including debt restructuring, cost rationalisation, operational turnaround, and NCLT/IBC-related advisory.

MIS & Management Reporting

Design and implementation of Management Information Systems (MIS), financial dashboards, KPI tracking, and periodic management reporting for business leaders.
