Our Scope

Tax Planning Services We Offer

Comprehensive tax planning and compliance services for every taxpayer category

Individual Tax Planning

Comprehensive tax planning for salaried individuals, professionals, and HUFs — optimising deductions under Sections 80C, 80D, HRA, LTA, and other provisions.

Business Tax Planning

Tax-efficient structuring of business income, choice of optimal tax regime, depreciation planning, and deduction maximisation for firms, LLPs, and companies.

Capital Gains Tax Planning

Strategic planning for capital gains from property, shares, and mutual funds — including reinvestment planning under Sections 54, 54EC, 54F to minimise tax incidence.

ITR Filing (All Categories)

Accurate and timely filing of all ITR forms — ITR-1 through ITR-7 — for individuals, HUFs, firms, companies, trusts, and other entity types.

TDS Compliance

End-to-end TDS management including deduction calculations, payment, return filing (24Q, 26Q, 27Q), and TDS certificate issuance to ensure zero default penalties.

Income Tax Notices

Expert handling of income tax notices, scrutiny assessments (Sec 143(3), 148), faceless appeals, and CIT(A) appeals with comprehensive written submissions.
