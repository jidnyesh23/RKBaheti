What We Do

Robust GST Dispute Resolution

Receiving a GST notice or facing a departmental audit can be stressful and legally complex. RKBaheti & Associates brings deep expertise in GST litigation to represent your case at every level — from responding to show-cause notices to arguing appeals before the Appellate Authority, AAR/AAAR, and High Courts.

Our litigation team combines technical knowledge with strategic thinking to build strong, well-documented cases. We ensure every deadline is met, every argument is supported by law and precedence, and your liability exposure is minimised.

Our Scope

Litigation Services We Offer

End-to-end GST dispute resolution and litigation support

Notice Reply & SCN Response

Drafting legally sound and factually accurate replies to GST notices, scrutiny queries, and Show Cause Notices to establish a strong defence from the outset.

Personal Hearing Representation

Representing your interests at personal hearings before Assessing Officers, Additional Commissioners, and Commissioners with well-prepared written submissions.

First & Second Appeals

Filing and arguing first appeals before the Appellate Authority and second appeals before GSTAT with comprehensive written briefs and legal arguments.

Advance Ruling (AAR)

Filing applications before the Authority for Advance Ruling (AAR) and Appellate AAR to get binding rulings on complex GST classification and compliance questions.

Audit & Inspection Support

Complete support during departmental audits, inspections, and survey operations — ensuring minimal disruption and proper documentation of your defence.

High Court Matters

Assistance in preparing writ petitions and coordinating with senior advocates for High Court matters on constitutional and jurisdictional GST issues.
