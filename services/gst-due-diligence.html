What We Do

Uncover Hidden GST Risks & Liabilities

Before entering into any business transaction — be it a merger, acquisition, joint venture, or investment — understanding the GST compliance health of the target entity is critical. Undisclosed GST liabilities can significantly impact deal valuations and create post-transaction complications.

Our GST due-diligence service offers a comprehensive, structured review of the target company's GST records, filings, ITC claims, and regulatory compliance. We produce a detailed report highlighting risks, contingent liabilities, and recommended remediation actions.

  • Pre-acquisition GST compliance review
  • ITC eligibility & reversal risk analysis
  • Supply classification & rate risk assessment
  • Pending notices, orders & litigation review
  • Reconciliation of books with GST returns
  • Detailed risk-rated due-diligence report

Our Scope

Due-Diligence Coverage Areas

A structured, comprehensive review covering all critical GST compliance aspects

Registration Review

Verification of GST registration details, multi-state GSTIN compliance, amendment history, and identification of any registration-related risks.

ITC Review & Risk

Analysis of ITC availed vs eligible ITC, identification of ineligible credits, reversal requirements, and assessment of ITC-related contingent liabilities.

Classification & Rate Review

Assessment of HSN/SAC classification accuracy, applicable GST rates, and identification of rate classification disputes or errors.

Notices & Litigation Review

Review of all pending notices, ongoing litigation, disputed demands, and assessment orders to quantify legal and financial exposure.

Return Reconciliation

Cross-verification of data in GST returns with financial books, identification of discrepancies, and assessment of potential demand exposure.

Due-Diligence Report

Comprehensive, structured report with risk classification (high/medium/low), quantification of liabilities, and actionable management recommendations.
