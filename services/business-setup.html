Our Scope

Corporate Services We Offer

From incorporation to ongoing corporate compliance — complete solutions

Company Incorporation

End-to-end incorporation of Private Limited Companies, One Person Companies, and Section 8 Companies — DIN, DSC, MOA, AOA, and Certificate of Incorporation.

LLP Registration

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) formation, LLP Agreement drafting, DPIN registration, and ongoing annual compliance — ideal for professionals and MSMEs.

ROC Annual Filings

Timely filing of AOC-4 (Financial Statements), MGT-7 (Annual Return), and other MCA forms to keep your company in good standing with the Registrar of Companies.

Board Meetings & Resolutions

Preparation of Board Meeting agenda, minutes, and resolutions; maintenance of statutory books; Annual General Meeting compliance and documentation.

Entity Restructuring

Conversion of entities (Proprietorship to Pvt Ltd, Firm to LLP), share transfers, director changes, registered office changes, and company name changes.

Registrations & Licenses

PAN, TAN, GST, Shops & Establishment, Professional Tax, Import-Export Code (IEC), FSSAI, and other business registrations and licenses at state/central level.
