Our Scope

Audit Services We Offer

Comprehensive audit and assurance services for every business requirement

Statutory Audit

Independent statutory audit of financial statements for companies, LLPs, and large firms as required under Companies Act, 2013 and applicable standards on auditing.

Tax Audit (Sec 44AB)

Mandatory tax audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act, including Form 3CA/3CB and Form 3CD preparation with complete disclosure compliance.

GST Audit (GSTR-9C)

Reconciliation of annual GST returns (GSTR-9) with audited financial statements and preparation of GSTR-9C with certified reconciliation statement.

Internal Audit

Risk-based internal audit of business processes, financial controls, and operational procedures with detailed management reports and improvement recommendations.

Concurrent Audit

Ongoing concurrent audit of transactions and processes — particularly for financial institutions, NBFCs, and high-volume businesses requiring real-time monitoring.

Special Purpose Audits

Agreed-upon procedure engagements, limited reviews, forensic accounting, and special purpose audit reports for specific legal or regulatory requirements.
